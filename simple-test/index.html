Hover Effects TS Demo

Zoom Effect

Hover over the image to see a magnification effect that follows your cursor.

applyHoverEffect(element, {
  effect: "zoom",
  scale: 1.5,
  radius: 120
});

ASCII Effect

Hover over the image to see it transform into ASCII characters.

applyHoverEffect(element, {
  effect: "ascii",
  size: 12,
  radius: 80
});

Particle Dust Effect

Hover over the image to see particles disperse around your cursor.

applyHoverEffect(element, {
  effect: "particle-dust",
  spacing: 4,
  maxDrift: 30,
  radius: 100
});

Pixel Effect

Hover over the image to see pixels appear around your cursor.

applyHoverEffect(element, {
  effect: "pixel",
  blockSize: 6,
  radius: 120
});

Minecraft Effect

Hover over the image to see 3D blocks appear around your cursor.

applyHoverEffect(element, {
  effect: "minecraft",
  blockSize: 8,
  radius: 120
});
